What's the Difference?

Understand the key difference between Aadhaar Linking and DBT Seeding for scholarship payments.

Aadhaar Linking

Just connecting your Aadhaar number to your bank account

  • • Needed for KYC
  • • Does not enable DBT payments
  • • Most students have this only

DBT Seeding (Aadhaar Enabled)

Making your account ready to receive government benefits

  • • Needed for DBT payments
  • • Must be requested at bank
  • • Enables scholarships & subsidies

Key Point: Linking ≠ Seeding!

Most students have linking but are missing seeding. That's why scholarships fail!
